====================================
Welcome to the Cordys JMS Connector
====================================
The JMS connector is meant for connecting to JMS middleware like Websphere MQ, Sun J2EE, and others. 
The JMS connector includes functionality such as; JMS 1.1 support, support for queue's and topics, 
read/write and triggering on destinations (a destination is either a queue or a topic), transaction support,
support of different message formats, like clear text, XML, base64 and Binary Transformation Channel (BTC) protocols, 
and request/reply with optional use of correlation id.
